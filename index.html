A Little Bit About Me

My name is Tereza. I have lived in the Netherlands since 2014 and for most of that time I worked for an accommodation search tech company in various positions in their Workfoce Management Department. At one point I realized however that I didn't see myself in this field of work longterm and I didn't feel passionate about the job. I started to learn how to code and eventually quit my job to attend a coding bootcamp in Amsterdam called Le Wagon which I recently finished. I took me a while to figure out what I want to do but now I belive coding is the right thing for me. It is something I feel passionate about and I am excited for the future.

Things I have built so far
VayCar

A group project that I worked on at Le Wagon with three other students. We had a week to build a market place web app with Ruby on Rails, and we created a platform for airport car parking and hire. We developed both car owner and car renter journeys - a user could list their car for rent at an airport or look for cars to rent, send a request to the owner, and leave a review after they used the car.

Check it out
BetaVision

A final two-week Ruby on Rails project I worked on with three other students at Le Wagon. It is a platform where companies can sign up and upload a beta version of their website/ app, select what their target market through multiple filters and get feedback from users who registered as testers. Companies pay per test. After the feedback is received, they can give a review to the tester based on how useful their feedback was.

Check it out
Boozy Town

My first solo Ruby on Rails project created at Le Wagon. It is a cocktail recipe manager app where a user can add a cocktail with photo to the collectionn, view specifics of other cocktails: ingredients and their amounts, reviews, or they can add or remove ingredients and leave a review to already existing cocktails.
